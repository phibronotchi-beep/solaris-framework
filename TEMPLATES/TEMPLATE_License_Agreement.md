**3.2 Royalty Payments**

**Royalty Rate:** [X]% of Net Revenue

- Returns and refunds
- Sales taxes and duties

- R&D costs

- General overhead

**3.3 Royalty Reports**

- Total units sold

- Net revenue
- Royalty calculation
- Payment confirmation

**4.3 Termination at Will**

- Royalty obligations continue for existing sales

## NOTES ON THIS TEMPLATE

### Before Using:

2. Choose options where indicated
3. Customize Section 15 for unique terms

5. Keep signed copy for records

### This Is a Starting Point:
- Simple enough for small deals
- Comprehensive enough for enforceability
- Can be customized for complexity
- Not a substitute for legal advice

### What Makes This "Simple":
- Plain language
- 2-3 pages core terms + schedules
- Standard provisions
- Focus on clarity

### For Complex Situations, Consider Attorney If:
- Deal value >$500K
- Multiple parties
- International elements
- Patents involved
- Major custom terms
